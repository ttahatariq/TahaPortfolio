Game Development (Adventure of Taha 2D game)

For my semester project in game development, I embarked on an exciting journey to create a 2D game titled "Adventure of Taha." This captivating game took players on an epic adventure as they guided the protagonist through mystical gates into an alien world, filled with unique challenges and adversaries to overcome. Using Unity as the game engine, I harnessed a diverse set of skills to bring this imaginative world to life.

The development of "Adventure of Taha" demanded proficiency in Unity's game development framework, encompassing aspects like character movement, level design, and collision detection. It also required scripting in C# to implement game mechanics, character behavior, and interactions with the alien creatures. Visual assets, animations, and sound effects were incorporated to create an immersive gaming experience.

In addition to technical skills, project management and creativity played pivotal roles. I had to plan and organize the game's development process, balance gameplay, and design captivating levels that engaged players. The project highlighted my ability to work within a multidisciplinary team, as game development often requires collaboration among artists, programmers, and designers.

"Adventure of Taha" not only showcased my technical prowess in Unity but also demonstrated my passion for game development and my capacity to craft an engaging and captivating gaming experience. It was a testament to my dedication to honing my skills in this dynamic and innovative field.

Skills: Unity · C# · Character Animation
