Project information

  • Category: Final Year Project
  • Client: COMSATS University Islamabad
  • Project date: Sep 2022 - Jul 2023

CUI Help Desk

  • CUI Help Desk is a social interplay app for student-to-student and student-to-instructor interaction.
  • University students can get help from different students and teachers by means of posting their queries.
  • Students or teachers can reply to queries in comments and private messages.
  • The app enables direct engagement with instructors, addressing limitations of other platforms.
  • Teachers can submit learning resources, and students can access categorized resources.
  • Students can filter categories for specific learning resources.
  • Teachers can select interests to receive relevant queries.
  • The app aids students in understanding admissions requirements and application processes.
  • Students can access admission-related queries, dates, merit lists, and more.
  • Overall, CUI Help Desk enhances student-to-student and student-to-instructor interaction at COMSATS University Islamabad.
